# 🌱 Vridhi Mitra – Complete Feature List
### AI-Based Student Performance & Personalized Learning System

---

## 🔐 Authentication & Security
- **JWT-based Login** – Secure login with email and password
- **User Registration with OTP Verification** – Email-based OTP (via Nodemailer) to verify new accounts
- **Role-Based Access Control** – Three roles: Student, Faculty, Admin — each with tailored dashboards
- **Protected Routes** – Automatic redirection based on user role; unauthorized access blocked
- **Session Persistence** – Token stored in localStorage for seamless login across sessions

## 📡 API Endpoints (6 Route Modules)

### Authentication (`/api/auth`)
- `POST /register` – Register with OTP
- `POST /verify-otp` – Verify email
- `POST /login` – JWT login

### Student (`/api/student`)
- `GET /dashboard` – Aggregated stats & charts
- `GET /performance` – Marks history
- `GET /assessments` – Pending & completed
- `POST /assessments/:id/submit` – Submit quiz
- `GET /attendance` – Attendance records
- `GET /learning-paths` – AI study plans
- `GET /materials` – Course materials
- `GET /achievements` – Badges
- `GET /notifications` – Alerts

### Faculty (`/api/faculty`)
- `GET /dashboard` – Faculty overview
- `POST /courses` – Create course
- `POST /assessments` – Create assessment
- `POST /marks` – Bulk marks entry
- `POST /attendance` – Bulk attendance
- `POST /courses/:id/materials` – Upload DCM
- `GET /export/analytics` – Excel download

### Admin (`/api/admin`)
- `GET /dashboard` – System stats
- `GET/PUT/DELETE /users` – User management
- `POST /courses` – Course management
- `POST /enrollments` – Enrollment management

### AI (`/api/ai`)
- `POST /chat` – Pragati Bot conversation
- `POST /analyze` – Performance analysis
- `POST /quiz` – Quiz generation

### Discussions (`/api/discussions`)
- `GET/POST /` – List & create posts
- `POST /:id/replies` – Reply to post
- `POST /:id/upvote` – Upvote post
